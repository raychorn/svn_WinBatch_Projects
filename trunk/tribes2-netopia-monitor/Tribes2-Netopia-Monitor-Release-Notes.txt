Quick readme on this release of Tribes2-Netopia-Monitor - The Tribes2 Utility to monitor Players and Bandwidth usage.

-------------------
Installation
-------------------

Make a folder called C:\Tribes2-Netopia-Monitor or the like and then double click Tribes2-Netopia-Monitor.exe
