Hosting

In the last 18 Months we have tried many hosts, with 12 sites of various sorts you get a good sample of the services available:-

It is worth relaying some of our experiences for your benefit! These comments are based on our experiences and may not be representative of the general services offered.

RIP OFFS - Avoid or treat with EXTREME CAUTION!

PBTech -  3 months up front + set up fees. The site took ages to set up and then was very SLOW! Complaints were answered slowly and then the site speeded up! Only problem was that then the Site refused to allow downloads of any files above 60Kb! Useless!
We were promised a refund of our US$370 but are still waiting! - Basically a RIP OFF!

WideXS - Almost identical to PBtech, also based in Holland, servers in US. OK at first but watch the performance drop off as the server OVERLOADS! It was taking 30secs to download a 10Kb file at 33.6 - again no refund!

EXPENSIVE - You may not realise

Tri Star Web  - Good servers and very Reliable - but watch their business practises. What you sign up for may not be what you get. We agreed in great detail 60Gb a month over three domains for US$175 per month. Paid by Credit Card. Very soon we found they changed the rules, the $175 a month only covered 18Gb and then without permission they charged the balance for excess bandwidth to the Card! Total was over US$450 a month for 55Gb!

Cybercash - Smalltalker - Looks like an incredible deal and the servers are FAST! Watch out though, the cheap webspace quickly gets filled with LOG FILES, the promised WEBTRENDS analysis software just isn't there and you get billed WEEKLY at $1 per Mb over the original space and $9 per Gb over the Bandwidth, all billed to your credit card, even if you tell them not to! To upgrade incurs a Setup charge of $50 min every time. We signed up on 22nd of Month and paid a FULL months hosting fees but where reinvoiced on 13th of next month for a full month, i.e. the initial full month was 22nd - end of month! Asking for the PROMISED REFUND has failed to yeild a result, all you get is smart answers! Total charged to the credit card over three weeks was - $480+ Bandwidth was almost certainly less than 60Gb! ( Based on Previous host stats at a higher hit rate! )  You also get hit by massive amounts of email notices, over 100 including answers to support questions (10),  in less than three weeks!

SLOW and UNRELIABLE

VIRTUALynx - In Canada - the service is good and the staff helpful, it isn't really them but the location of the server. Links to Canada are POOR leaving you with a server that appears Slow and Unreliable. See www.internettrafficreport.com

BJSquared - service decayed rapidly as our site got going. We assumed the owners site always got preference, couldn't prove it but our site was down a lot at busy times whilst BJSquared stayed up! Too SMALL to be a real service!

Quantum Group - After a month of trying, with massive downtimes we gave up! No charge but the cost in lost traffic was incredible! Support was useless!

We repeat that these are purely observations based on our experience with the hosts in question, they may have improved their services or our experience may have been a one-off position. However we feel that it is worth publishing this data, if a host listed above feels the report is unfair or does not represent the position accurately or has improved dramatically please contact us and we will publish amendments accordingly!
