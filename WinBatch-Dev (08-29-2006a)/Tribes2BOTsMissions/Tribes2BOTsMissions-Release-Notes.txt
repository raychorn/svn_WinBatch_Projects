Quick readme on this release of Tribes2BOTsMissions.EXE - The Tribes2 Utility to Copy BOTs Missions from one folder to another.

-------------------
Installation
-------------------

Make a folder under C:\Program Files or the like and then double click Tribes2BOTsMissions.exe

You will be asked for your Tribes2 Destination Root Folder and then your Tribes2 Source Root Folder.

Tribes2BOTsMissions then copies all your BOTs Missions from the Source Folders to the Destination Folders but will prompt you whenever it may overwrite an existing file in the Desitnation folders.
